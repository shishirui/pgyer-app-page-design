About this App

Duolingo is the world's most popular language learning platform. Learn Spanish, French, German, Italian, Russian, Portuguese, Turkish, Dutch, Irish, Danish, Swedish, Ukrainian, Esperanto, Polish, Greek, Hungarian, Norwegian, Hebrew, Welsh, Arabic, Latin, Hawaiian, Scottish Gaelic, Vietnamese, Korean, Japanese, English, and even High Valyrian!

With fun, bite-sized lessons that feel like a game, you'll build solid skills across reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Our courses are built by the best language-learning experts, and powered by personalized learning that adapts to your level and pace. Plus, it's free!

What's New in v2.4.0

NEW: AI-powered conversation practice with real-time feedback. Improved pronunciation scoring with advanced speech recognition. Added listening exercises with native speakers from around the world. Enhanced streak rewards and daily goal challenges. Performance improvements and bug fixes.
